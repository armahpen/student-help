Our Tutoring Services

We offer comprehensive tutoring programs for students at every stage of their academic journey.

Primary School (Grades 1-6)

Build a strong foundation with tutoring in math, reading, writing, and science for young learners aged 6-12.

Learn More

Our Primary School program includes one-on-one sessions to address individual learning gaps, interactive group activities, and homework support. Subjects covered include basic arithmetic, phonics, creative writing, and introductory science experiments. Sessions are designed to boost confidence and prepare students for higher grades.

  • Duration: 1-hour sessions, 2-3 times per week
  • Special Features: Interactive online tools and printable worksheets

High School (Grades 7-12)

Excel in subjects like algebra, biology, chemistry, and English with our targeted high school tutoring programs.

Learn More

Our High School program offers advanced tutoring to help students master challenging curricula, prepare for exams (e.g., SAT, GCSE), and develop study skills. We provide subject-specific support with certified tutors specializing in STEM and humanities. Additionally, we offer a special service during school vacations and breaks to fill learning gaps, ensuring students return refreshed and improved.

  • Duration: 1.5-hour sessions, 2-4 times per week
  • Special Features: Exam preparation workshops, personalized study plans, and vacation gap-filling sessions

University

Get support for advanced courses, including calculus, physics, literature, and more, tailored to university students.

Learn More

Our University program supports students with complex coursework, thesis preparation, and exam revision. Tutors are subject matter experts with advanced degrees, offering customized lessons to meet academic and career goals.

  • Duration: 2-hour sessions, 1-3 times per week
  • Special Features: One-on-one thesis guidance and career advice
